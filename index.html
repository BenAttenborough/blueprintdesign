About Me

After many years in journalism I decided I wanted a career change so I came to the Cambridge School of Art to study for an MA in Graphic Design and Typography.

The course has enabled me to really stretch my wings, designing innovative print layouts, custom typefaces and photographic projects on one hand and digital creations including app design on the other.

For my final major project I have created a walking tour app for the Mill Road History Project. This app connects the physical environment to historical information and allows people to experience history in the place where it happened. It also explores the relationship between official histories and the folk memories which make up the culture of an area.

I have been doing voluntary graphic design work for the Museum of Technology in Cambridge and I am excited about the career possibilities that have opened up for me.
