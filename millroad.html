About Me

Welcome to my blog charting my final major project for a masters in graphic design and typography at Anglia Ruskin University.

My intention with this project is to investigate how history can be represented in a digital format. I intend to investigate the benefits of using mobile technology in the presentation of history.

I intend to create an app which users can use to discover more about the history of their location as they explore it for real.

The aim is to create a new way for people to learn about and understand history by using geography as a starting point.

See the current live version of this project (demonstrated on the desktop) here: http://millroadproject.businesscatalyst.com/
